Wireless CAR

i have made wireless car using NRF MODULES having Range of 400 Meter


- How It Works:

1. Wireless Transmission:The transmitter, powered by an NRF module, sends signals wirelessly to the receiver unit.
2. Receiver Activation: Upon receiving signals, the receiver unit triggers relays to activate corresponding electrical components.
3. Arduino Integration: Both the transmitter and receiver units are powered and controlled by Arduino microcontrollers, allowing for precise signal interpretation and relay activation.
